Gaoshan Nationality

Introduction of Gaoshan Nationality

The Gaoshan nationality is a general term for all ethnic groups of
the Nandao language family in Taiwan, China, China. It refers to the
population of the "Chinese nation" other than the Han nationality and
other mainland nationalities on the island and its affiliated islands. Its
scope is roughly equivalent to the so-called "Shengfan" of the Han
nationality in history, and the so-called "Tibetan" and "Gaosha
nationality" of the Japanese colonial authorities.
