Custom in War

Taiwan Gaoshan people are brave and good at fighting.
They have good physical strength and physical coordination
ability, can make full use of bows and arrows and sharp knives,
and can also use the advantages of terrain to fight with the enemy
for a long time. When defeated by the enemy, the customs of the
Gaoshan ethnic group will make the men in the ethnic group not shrink
back until the last person dies, while the old women will hang
themselves to save their own zun
